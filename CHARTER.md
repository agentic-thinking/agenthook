# Charter

This Charter is the binding statement of stewardship terms under which Agentic Thinking Limited publishes the AgentHook specification. It supplements [`GOVERNANCE.md`](./GOVERNANCE.md) and is incorporated into it by reference.

---

**Charter version:** 1.0

**Status:** Binding on the steward and any successor steward.

---

## 1. Specification licence

The AgentHook specification, including all text, schemas, examples, and test materials in this repository, is and shall remain licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0.

The steward shall not, and shall not authorise any successor to, replace, restrict, supplement, or revoke this licence in respect of any version of the specification published under it.

## 2. No royalty

No fee may be charged by the steward or any successor for:

- the right to read, copy, redistribute, or implement the specification;
- the right to claim conformance with the specification, where such conformance has been verified by the conformance test suite;
- the right to use the name "AgentHook" in describing a conforming implementation, subject to the trademark terms set out in section 5.

## 3. Patent grant

The steward, on behalf of itself and its assigns, grants to every party that implements the specification a worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive, irrevocable patent licence to make, have made, use, sell, offer for sale, import, and otherwise transfer any conforming implementation, in respect of any patent claim owned or controlled by the steward that would necessarily be infringed by such implementation in compliance with the specification.

This grant survives any change of stewardship.

## 4. Right to fork

Any party may fork the specification under the Apache License 2.0. The steward shall not assert trademark, copyright, or patent rights against forks, save that forks shall not represent themselves as the official AgentHook specification or claim conformance unless verified by the conformance test suite operated by the steward (or successor body).

## 5. Trademarks

AgentHook™ is a trade mark of Agentic Thinking Limited.

The AgentHook name shall be made available for nominative use by conforming implementations under terms no more restrictive than the Apache 2.0 patent grant in section 3. Use that misrepresents non-conforming implementations as conforming, or that claims affiliation with the steward without the steward's permission, is reserved.

## 6. Stewardship transfer

The steward intends to transfer stewardship to a neutral foundation when adoption of the specification justifies the legal and operational cost of formal foundation governance. Triggers that the steward considers material to such transfer include:

- five or more independent organisations are publicly conformant at any tier;
- the specification is cited in regulatory guidance by the European Commission, a Member State competent authority, or an equivalent jurisdiction;
- annual revenue from conformance certification activities exceeds the operational cost of running a neutral foundation under standard charity or non-profit accounting in the United Kingdom.

Until such transfer, the steward shall continue to operate under the terms of this Charter and the Governance document. Transfer shall preserve all licence and patent grants made under this Charter and shall be effected in a manner that does not impair any existing implementation's rights.

## 7. Amendment

This Charter may be amended only by:

- unanimous approval of the Working Group as constituted under [`GOVERNANCE.md`](./GOVERNANCE.md), and
- twelve months' prior public notice published in this repository,

except that editorial corrections that do not change the substance of any term may be merged with the approval of two Working Group members and announced in a regular release note.

## 8. Severability

If any provision of this Charter is held to be unenforceable in a given jurisdiction, the remaining provisions remain in full force.
