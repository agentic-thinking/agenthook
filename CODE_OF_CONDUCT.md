# Code of Conduct

The AgentHook Working Group adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1, in full.

## Scope

This Code applies in all Working Group spaces, including this repository, GitHub Discussions, Issues, the monthly meetings, and any other forum operated under the AgentHook name. It also applies when an individual is officially representing the Working Group in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the steward at contact@agenticthinking.uk. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The Working Group is committed to confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident. Further details of specific enforcement policies may be posted separately.

The steward serves as the initial enforcement point per [`GOVERNANCE.md`](./GOVERNANCE.md).

For reports involving the steward or any of its officers, contractors, or directors, please escalate to the Independent Ombudsman per [`CHARTER.md`](./CHARTER.md) section 9. The Independent Ombudsman will be identified and appointed imminently. Until appointed, reports involving the steward may be filed as a private issue marked for the maintainers, or sent to the steward with a clear request that the matter be escalated to the Working Group rather than handled internally.

The Working Group may elect an additional independent enforcement panel at any time.
